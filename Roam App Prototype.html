Roam+ Membership

Everything's still full price. This just makes the trip smoother.

Roam never marks up a flight, hotel, or tour — that doesn't change. Roam+ is a membership for travelers who want a few extras layered on top, and it's how Roam stays in business without touching the price you pay partners.

$59/year (~$5/month)
Demo only — this is a class project prototype, no real payment is processed.

Zero booking-assistance fees

Free plan trips carry a small per-trip service fee. Roam+ waives it entirely.

Price-drop protection

If your flight or hotel rate drops after you book, Roam+ credits you the difference.

Flexible-date overlay

Free cancellation / date-change coverage layered on top of eligible bookings.

Early access to flash deals

See partner discount codes and limited-time fares before free-plan users do.

Unlimited trip customization

Free plan caps flight/hotel/activity swaps per trip. Roam+ removes the cap.

Ad-free, unbiased results

Free plan may show a sponsored placement among your options. Roam+ never does.

Group trip & shared itineraries

Share an editable itinerary with travel companions, not just a read-only link.

Priority human support

Jump the queue if you ever need help from a real person.
